## Description (4000 characters max)

RunWalk is a simple, beautiful interval timer designed for run-walk training. Perfect for marathon training, Couch to 5K programs, or anyone looking to build endurance through alternating run and walk intervals.

**NOW WITH GPS TRACKING & STATISTICS**
Track your routes with live maps, monitor your progress with detailed statistics, and use Siri to start your workouts hands-free.

**APPLE WATCH**
Run your intervals right from your wrist - no iPhone required. Get distinct haptic patterns so you can feel the transitions, see your heart rate zone in real-time, and your workouts automatically count toward your Activity Rings.

**SIMPLE & FOCUSED**
No complicated setup. Just select your interval and tap Start. The app announces "Run!" and "Walk!" at each transition so you can keep your phone in your pocket.

**WORKOUT PRESETS**
Choose from built-in presets or create your own:
- Easy Start - Perfect for beginners
- Beginner - Classic run-walk ratio
- Intermediate - Building endurance
- Advanced - For experienced runners
- Custom presets you create

**INTERVAL OPTIONS**
Choose from 6 research-based intervals used by top running coaches:
- 30 seconds - Great for beginners
- 1 minute - Classic run-walk ratio
- 1.5 minutes - Couch to 5K style
- 2 minutes - Intermediate training
- 3 minutes - Building endurance
- 5 minutes - Advanced runners
- Custom intervals from 10 seconds to 30 minutes

**GPS & ROUTE TRACKING**
- Live map shows your route as you run
- Distance tracking with pace calculation
- Route maps saved with workout history
- Swipe between timer and map during workout

**STATISTICS & PROGRESS**
- Track your workout streaks
- Weekly and monthly workout charts
- All-time statistics
- Progress over time

**SIRI SHORTCUTS**
- "Hey Siri, start run walk workout"
- "Hey Siri, start Beginner workout with RunWalk"
- Works with Shortcuts app for custom automations

**HEART RATE ZONES (APPLE WATCH)**
- Live heart rate display during workout
- 5-zone system matching Apple's workout app
- See your zone at a glance (e.g., "129 Z2")
- Set your age in Settings for accurate zones

**DESIGNED FOR RUNNERS**
- Large, easy-to-read display
- Works with screen locked
- Dark theme reduces glare outdoors
- Haptic feedback for silent alerts
- Customizable settings for bells, voice, and haptics
- No account required
- No ads, no tracking

**THE RUN-WALK METHOD**
The run-walk method (popularized by Olympic runner Jeff Galloway) is a proven technique that helps runners:
- Reduce fatigue and injury risk
- Run longer distances
- Recover faster
- Enjoy running more

Whether you're training for your first 5K or your tenth marathon, RunWalk keeps your intervals simple so you can focus on your run.
