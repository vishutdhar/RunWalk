## Description (4000 characters max)

RunWalk is a simple, beautiful interval timer designed for run-walk training. Perfect for marathon training, Couch to 5K programs, or anyone looking to build endurance through alternating run and walk intervals.

**NOW ON APPLE WATCH**
Run your intervals right from your wrist - no iPhone required. Get distinct haptic patterns so you can feel the transitions, and your workouts automatically count toward your Activity Rings.

**SIMPLE & FOCUSED**
No complicated setup. Just select your interval and tap Start. The app announces "Run!" and "Walk!" at each transition so you can keep your phone in your pocket.

**VOICE ANNOUNCEMENTS**
Clear voice cues tell you when to run and when to walk. No need to look at your phone or watch while exercising.

**POPULAR INTERVAL OPTIONS**
Choose from 6 research-based intervals used by top running coaches:
• 30 seconds - Great for beginners
• 1 minute - Classic run-walk ratio
• 1.5 minutes - Couch to 5K style
• 2 minutes - Intermediate training
• 3 minutes - Building endurance
• 5 minutes - Advanced runners

**DESIGNED FOR RUNNERS**
• Large, easy-to-read display
• Works with screen locked
• Dark theme reduces glare outdoors
• Haptic feedback for silent alerts
• Customizable settings for bells, voice, and haptics
• No account required
• No ads, no tracking

**APPLE WATCH FEATURES**
• Standalone app - works without your iPhone
• Runs in background when wrist is lowered
• Distinct haptic patterns for run vs walk
• Workouts save to Health and count toward Activity Rings
• Workout history on your wrist

**THE RUN-WALK METHOD**
The run-walk method (popularized by Olympic runner Jeff Galloway) is a proven technique that helps runners:
• Reduce fatigue and injury risk
• Run longer distances
• Recover faster
• Enjoy running more

Whether you're training for your first 5K or your tenth marathon, RunWalk keeps your intervals simple so you can focus on your run.
