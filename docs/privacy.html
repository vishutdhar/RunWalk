Privacy Policy for RunWalk

Last Updated: January 5, 2025

Overview

RunWalk is a simple interval timer designed for run-walk training. Your privacy is important to us, and we want to be transparent about our data practices.

We do not collect any personal data.

Data Collection

The App:

  • Does NOT collect personal information
  • Does NOT track your location
  • Does NOT access your contacts, photos, or other personal data
  • Does NOT use analytics or tracking services
  • Does NOT contain advertisements
  • Does NOT require an account or sign-in
  • Does NOT transmit any data over the internet

Data Storage

All app settings (such as your selected interval duration) are stored locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third parties.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party services, SDKs, or analytics platforms.

Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any data from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last Updated" date above.
